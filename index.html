💪 Morning-Focused HIIT & 🏋️ Weighted Workout Plan with 🍽️ Diet

🎯 Goal: Increase weight from 73 kg to 85 kg (Lean Muscle Gain)

🔥 Morning Workout Plan (6 Days/Week)

Each day includes HIIT (45s on, 15s rest) and weighted exercises (4 sets of 8-12 reps). Sundays are for active recovery.

Day 1: Full Body Strength

  • HIIT: Jumping Jacks, Burpees, Mountain Climbers
  • Weighted: Deadlifts, Squats, Bench Press

Day 2: Lower Body Power

  • HIIT: Jump Squats, Step-Ups, Glute Bridges
  • Weighted: Leg Press, Calf Raises, Romanian Deadlifts

Day 3: Push (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps)

  • HIIT: Push-ups, Tricep Dips, Jump Rope
  • Weighted: Shoulder Press, Incline Dumbbell Press, Lateral Raises

Day 4: Pull (Back, Biceps)

  • HIIT: Plank to Push-up, Skater Jumps, High Knees
  • Weighted: Pull-Ups, Barbell Rows, Bicep Curls

Day 5: Core & Stability

  • HIIT: Russian Twists, Bicycle Crunches, Flutter Kicks
  • Weighted: Cable Woodchoppers, Hanging Leg Raises, Weighted Planks

Day 6: Power & Cardio Blast

  • HIIT: Sprints (100m x 5), Jump Rope, Sled Push
  • Weighted: Power Cleans, Push Press, Kettlebell Swings

Day 7: Active Recovery

Light yoga, stretching, and walking (30-45 minutes).
